How habits define success ?

In order to achieve success we often think that we should set goals but this strategy turn out to be inefficient, some of the reasons why are:

  • Winners and losers have the same goals.
  • achieving a goal is a momentary change
  • goals restrict your happiness
  • goals are the opposite of long-term progress

We should focus instead on building our systems which is made of atomic habits (good & bad) that compound in silence until crossing the a critical threshold and make a change

Our goal is to become the best versions of our-selves which requires continuous change. there are three levels of change:

  • outcome change
  • process change
  • identity change

the best type is the 3rd. As a matter of fact if we change the outcome (goal oriented) our identity stops us, on the other hand if we focus on changing who we are, everything else follow but how can we change our identity ?

each time we repeat a habit is casting a vote for the type of person doing the habit and change our believes about ourselves, and our identity determines the habits we perform creating a Feedback-loop.
